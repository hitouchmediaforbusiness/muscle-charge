Questions

Before you subscribe.

Do I need any equipment?

No. Every session uses bodyweight movements. A chair or wall is occasionally used for balance support, but nothing is required to purchase.

Is there a free trial?

No — Muscle Charge is available to paid subscribers only. Your subscription unlocks full access to the app and the 28-Day Transformation program.

Is this safe if I have joint issues or haven't trained in years?

Sessions are designed with lower-impact, controlled movement in mind. That said, if you have an existing injury or health condition, check with your doctor before starting any new exercise program.

How much time do I need each day?

Sessions run 7 to 15 minutes depending on the day and week of the program.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Cancellation is managed from your account settings inside the app or app store, depending on where you subscribed.
